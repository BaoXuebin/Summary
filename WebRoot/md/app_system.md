例如：

```[sql]
INSERT INTO SPT_APP_INFO(APP_CODE, APP_NAME, APP_URL, MAIN_URL, APP_STATUS, MAIN_DEPT, OTHER_DEPT, APP_DESC, CREATE_USER_ID, CREATE_USER_NAME, CREATE_DATE, OPER_USER_ID, OPER_USER_NAME, OPER_DATE)

GO
```
